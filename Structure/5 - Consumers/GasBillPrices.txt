<p>Gas bill prices have <strong>increased</strong>, in cash terms, for direct debit (up <strong>0.6%</strong> from 2020) customers since 2020. In the same period, gas bill prices have <strong>decreased</strong>, in cash terms, for prepayment (down <strong>6.0%</strong> from 2020) and standard credit customers (down<strong> 1.0%</strong> from 2020) .</p>
<p><strong>Direct Debit </strong>customers, in 2021, paid on average the <strong>lowest</strong>&nbsp;<strong>gas bills</strong> in Scotland, <strong>8.4% lower</strong>&nbsp;than&nbsp;<strong>standard credit</strong> customers who paid the most, and <strong>0.5% lower</strong>&nbsp;than <strong>prepayment </strong>customers.</p>
<p>Differences between bills in North and South Scotland are negligible, and both regions are marginally lower than the British average.</p>
