<p>Transport emissions have <strong>fallen</strong> from <strong>1998</strong>, going from <strong>16.5 million tonnes</strong> <strong>of CO2</strong> to <strong>14.8 million tonnes of CO2</strong> in <strong>2018</strong>, a <strong>reduction</strong> of <strong>10.3%</strong>.</p>
<p>Transport emissions were at their <strong>lowest</strong> in <strong>2013</strong>, at <strong>13.6 million tonnes of CO2.</strong></p>
<p>As a<strong> proportion</strong> of overall emissions, transport has <strong>increased</strong> from <strong>21.8%</strong> in <strong>1998</strong> to <strong>35.6% </strong>in <strong>2018</strong>.</p>
