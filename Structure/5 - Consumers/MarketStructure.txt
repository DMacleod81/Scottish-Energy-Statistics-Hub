<p>In the last ten years, the number of electricity and gas suppliers in the Scottish market increased substantially, meaning that consumers have greater choice of supplier.&nbsp; However, the&nbsp;<strong>largest six suppliers</strong>&nbsp;&ndash; Scottish Power, SSE, British Gas, E.ON, npower and EDF &ndash; still collectively dominate the market.&nbsp; They supply electricity and gas to&nbsp;<strong>80.1%</strong>&nbsp;in&nbsp;<strong>North Scotland</strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong>74.6%</strong>&nbsp;in&nbsp;<strong>South Scotland</strong>.&nbsp;&nbsp; Based on market shares, Ofgem classify other suppliers into medium and small &ndash; South Scotland have a higher share of both than North Scotland</p>
