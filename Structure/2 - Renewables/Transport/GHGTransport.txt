<p>Transport emissions have <strong>fallen</strong> from <strong>1998</strong>, going from <strong>16.5 million tonnes</strong> <strong>of CO2</strong> to <strong>10.3&nbsp;million tonnes of CO2</strong> in <strong>2020</strong>, a <strong>reduction</strong> of <strong>37.6%</strong>.</p>
<p>Transport emissions are now at their lowest level.</p>
<p>As a<strong> proportion</strong> of overall emissions, transport has <strong>increased</strong> from <strong>20.5%</strong> in <strong>1998</strong> to <strong>25.9% </strong>in <strong>2020</strong>.</p>
